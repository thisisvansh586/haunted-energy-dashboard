2. If you see a "Create Demo Home" option, click it
3. It will automatically create 5 devices for you

## What You'll See

Once you add these devices:
- **Total Power Usage** will show the combined wattage
- **Devices panel** will list all your devices
- You can **toggle states** (on/off/standby) by clicking the state button
- **Real-time updates** will show power consumption changes
- The **telemetry simulator** will generate realistic power fluctuations
- **Anomalies** may be detected (phantom loads, power spikes)
